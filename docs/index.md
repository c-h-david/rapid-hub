# RAPID

## Routing Application for Programmed Integration of Discharge

Advanced river network modeling software for estimating water flow across
thousands of river reaches. Efficient matrix-based computations. Adaptable to
any dentritic river network with basic connectivity information.

RAPID is used by leading research institutions and scientific organizations
worldwide.

A 35-year historical simulation produced with RAPID is used to provide a first
guess for discharge estimates by the international Surface Water and Ocean
Topography (SWOT) mission that is jointly developed by NASA and CNES.
Read more in the
[simulation paper](https://doi.org/10.1029/2019WR025287)
and the
[SWOT discharge paper](https://doi.org/10.1029/2021WR031614).

  src="https://climate.copernicus.eu/sites/default/files/custom-uploads/branding/ECMWF_Master_Logo_RGB.png"

/>

A 40-year historical simulation and its associated 15-day ensemble forecast for
every river in the world produced with RAPID support the ECMWF GEOGLOWS
Streamflow system.
Read more in the
[research paper](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.envsoft.2019.104501)
or on the
[GEOGLOWS ECMWF Streamflow website](https://geoglows.ecmwf.int/).
